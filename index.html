Chocolate Cake

Rich, moist, and ready in under an hour.

Prep Time: 45 mins

Serves: 8

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a 9-inch pan.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Stir in water, oil, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Mix in vinegar; batter will slightly foam.
  5. Pour into pan and bake 30–35 minutes.
  6. Cool completely before slicing.
